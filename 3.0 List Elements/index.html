Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 package) active dry yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg plus 1 egg yolk, at room temperature
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
  • ¾ teaspoon salt

For the filling:

  • ¼ cup (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
  • 2 Tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Combine the flour and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Knead the dough.
  5. Let the dough rise.
  6. Make the filling.
  7. Roll out the dough.
  8. Spread the filling over the dough.
  9. Roll up the dough.
  10. Cut the dough into rolls.
